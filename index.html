HackUF is currently a work in progress. You can find out more information by clicking on any of the links above or continue reading.

Why

Inspired in part by HackBU, the purpose of HackUF is to give UF students the tools to turn their ideas into reality.

How

We'll primarily be using Ruby on Rails to build web applications.

When

As of right now, I'm planning on a weekly meeting (1-2hrs depending on the subject) with bi-weekly "hackathons" (read: gatherings with little sleep and pizza). I'll also be holding weekly office hours if anyone has any sorts of questions and also to track progress/feedback.

Where

TBD.

Who

Anyone who wants to learn how to build web applications. No previous experience is required (but is recommended!)

Disclaimer: chillax, we're hacking in the "maker" sense not the "bad-guy hacker" sense.
